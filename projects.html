Education

Our team has been sponsoring children from poor backgrounds to ensure no child stays at home due to lack of school fees.We have also been contributing towards girl child empowerment especially in areas where girl child was neglected before

Community service

Under community service it was an old age home and orphanage. Two things that are related to orphanages are basic education and skill development .

Environmental sustainability

Keeping our planet healthy is a pressing matter. We are not doing it just for us, but also for the many generations of humans and animal species to come. We understand the importance of the Earth after us and everyday we are working towards it.

Eradication of hunger

The number of hungry people in the world remains unacceptably high despite expected recent effort that have pushed the figure below 1 billion. FAO estimates that the number of people who have suffered from chronic hunger in 2014-16 is approx. 793 million. Our organization empowers women and men in rural villages to sustainably overcome hunger and poverty. Members of the community accomplish this feat by mobilizing and fostering effective partnerships to engage local government.

Health and hygiene

Our efforts are in the direction where population will be healthy happy and have basic food facilities. Our volunteers are dedicated and trying to ensure that no child goes to bed empty stomach.Rural areas in part of different continents lack proper and readily available health care services.We have made this possible by bringing these services near them.Our health care volunteers have helped us in making this mission possible.

Growth and economy

Under growth and economy we can also promote social media and do our charity drives. Sambal Ayu in order to eliminate this evil assigns a team of Doctors and Volunteers to different villages spreading awareness about good health practices. The doctors hold free health camps wherein all the villagers are screened and given appropriate medication.

Awareness Campaigns

About Charity

The Hurningtone International Foundation is a nonprofit organization committed to Elevate poverty, Advocate on importance of Human Rights, care for orphans and empower youth in rural parts of the world to end systemic deprivation, poverty and hunger through a holistic approach to community development, education, and healthcare.
