ABOUT HURNINGTONE FOUNDATION

Hurningtone International Foundation was founded in 2003 by Father Hurningtone Paul's of the Holy Trinity Church in the United Kingdom.

The Hurningtone International Foundation is a nonprofit organization committed to Elevate poverty, Advocate on importance of Human Rights, care for orphans and empower youth in rural parts of the world to end systemic deprivation, poverty and hunger through a holistic approach to community development, education, and healthcare. We’re audacious in our commitment to surround the globe Human race with a complete support system. Because every Human deserves all basic human rights, love, and care. Our model is rooted in social ecological theory, and we seek to impact every aspect of Human development to ensure a complete ecosystem. We need to understand what success looks like over the long term, for individuals, families, and communities.

We value bringing community-based organizations together to network, share strategies and build capacity in areas of work including, home-based care, youth, LGBTIQ health and human rights, and ending violence against women and girls Although we give to the large NGOs and well known charities, the greater part of our funding goes direct to small grassroots organisations which the bigger charities tend to overlook, and where we feel the recipients will achieve value for money. We also offer our partners grants for general operations. We believe that unrestricted funding is vital for an organization’s health and growth. The projects we fund are spread across the globe and in total we have now given to projects in 57 countries – wherever we can help we do with African countries now being a place of interest.

OUR PROJECTS

Recognizing our geographic location and the power dynamic inherent to being an international foundation, our partnership approach centres the work of community-based organizations, and is grounded in human rights, social justice and solidarity, not charity hence being able to manage over 670 projects worldwide.

Education Programs:

Hurningtone International sponsored students receive sponsorship for an average period of 4 years, allowing the fund to monitor progress and apply educational stability where, in most cases, economic, social, health or personal issues often obstruct a girl’s access to education. We only sponsor the girl child education.

HealthCare:
